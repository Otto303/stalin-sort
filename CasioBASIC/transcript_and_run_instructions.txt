"Enter list:"?->List 1

Dim List 1->L
2->I
While 1<=L

If List 1[I]<List 1[I - 1]
Then
L-1->L
For (I + 1)->J To Dim List 1
List 1[J]->List 1[J - 1]

Next
Else
I+1->I
IfEnd
WhileEnd

L->Dim List 2
For 1->I To L
List 1[I]->List 2[I]
Next

List 2
